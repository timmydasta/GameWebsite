Build Number: 0.3

Date Released: 24/01/2020

Updates:

  • The player can now drop items locally.
  • Dropped items will now spawn beneath the player and despawn after a certain period of time.
  • Earned exp and levels are now added to the database.
  • The player can now pick up items.
  • Certain items, such as axes, will now respawn after a certain period of time if they are picked up.
  • Added an equipment menu.
  • Added a deposit box that will destroy all items.
  • The player can now train their fishing and mining skills.
