Build Number: 0.22

Date Released: 10/5/2021

Updates:

  • You can now smith Melee armour
  • You can now craft Archery armour

  • You can now teleport to the spawn using the home teleport spell in the spellbook menu.
  • All Equipment slots will no longer display their stack number / stack background unless there is an item equipped in that slot with more than one item in the stack number
