Build Number: 0.3

Date Released: 24/01/2020

Updates:

  • The player will now earn woodcutting exp / levels locally when cutting trees.
  • Trees will now be depleted of resources.
  • Trees will respawn after a short period.
  • The player will now recieve logs in their inventory locally when cutting trees.
  • If the player's inventory is full they will not be able to harvest any more resources.
  • Information about the items in the player's inventory are now being loaded in from the database.
  • The player can now learn about items in their inventory by hovering over it's icon
  • The player can now right-click an item to open the right-click menu

Bug Fixes:

  • The skilling menu will now scroll if the player's cursor is hovering over one of the skill icons
